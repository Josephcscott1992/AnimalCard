Hummingbird

Hummingbirds are considered the smallest birds in the world and are lighter than a dime!

Scientific Name: Trochilade
Average Length: 7.5cm-13cm
Average Lifespan: 3-5 years
Habitat: Forests, Deserts, Lowlands, and Mountaintops

Small bird with a slendebill, hummingbirds have bright colored feathers with variety of colors. They are named from the humming sound made by the beating of their wings.
